# Free Versus Paid Boundaries

Threadify Workflows is a lead-magnet and activation layer. Threadify remains the paid execution system.

## Free Public Workflow Layer

- workflow manifests
- adapter instructions
- approval gates
- schedule/status/cancel/reschedule orchestration
- manual/web fallback
- basic receipts
- structured feedback capture
- basic analytics readback where Threadify exposes it

## Paid Threadify Layer

- native post generation
- draft editing
- reply generation
- advanced analytics and winner strategy
- additional accounts
- additional platforms
- premium workflows
- hosted calendar polish
- deeper receipts and team controls

Public manifests may include premium capability stubs, but not private prompts or private execution logic.
